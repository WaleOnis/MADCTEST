    SERVICES


    MADC Foundation (hereinafter referred to as "NGO Service Provider") provides the following among other programs:

    01

    Health Prevention and Education Programs:

    The MADC Foundation offers health prevention and education programs focused on sexually transmitted diseases (STDs), with a strong emphasis on HIV prevention. These programs aim to raise awareness about the transmission, prevention, and management of STDs, providing essential information and resources to reduce the incidence and impact of these diseases in both Michigan and Africa.

    02

    Education and Scholarships for the Needy:

    The MADC Foundation provides educational support and scholarships to individuals in need. These initiatives help to bridge educational gaps, offering financial assistance to students who may otherwise be unable to afford schooling, thereby fostering academic achievement and future opportunities.

    03

    Community Development Aids:

    MADC is dedicated to supporting community development through various aids and initiatives. These efforts include infrastructure improvements, resource allocation, and capacity-building projects designed to enhance the quality of life and promote sustainable development in local communities.

    04

    Widows and Single Parental Care Assistants:

    Understanding the unique challenges faced by widows and single parents, MADC offers care and support services tailored to their needs. These programs provide financial assistance, counseling, and resources aimed at empowering these individuals, ensuring they have the support needed to thrive despite their circumstances.

    05

    Old People and Motherless Baby Care Assistants:

    The MADC Foundation is committed to the care and support of elderly individuals and motherless babies. Through dedicated programs, the foundation offers essential services such as healthcare, housing, and emotional support, ensuring these vulnerable populations receive the care and attention they deserve.

    06

    Agriculture and Other Manufacturing Sectors:

    The foundation also promotes collaboration and development in agriculture and other manufacturing sectors. By connecting businesses and providing relevant information, the MADC Foundation helps to enhance agricultural practices, improve manufacturing processes, and support economic development in these critical industries.

    About Us


    Michigan African Development Council
    M.A.D.C

    The Michigan Africa Development Council (hereinafter referred to as "NGO Service Provider") is a Non-for-Profit Organization domiciled in Lagos, Nigeria.
    Its goal is to foment business ties between private and public companies in Michigan and throughout African countries with emphasis in West Africa. Michigan companies, and subsequently the economy of the state will benefit from exposure to underserved African opportunities in emergent countries. The Michigan Africa Development Council (MADC) promotes Michigan businesses and opportunities in Africa.



    Africa is abundant with a diverse array of valuable resources.
